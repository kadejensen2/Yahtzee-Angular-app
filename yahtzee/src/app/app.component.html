If you score more than 63 points on the number section you will earn an extra 35 points. A second Yahtzee can be used as a wild card for any of the categories, given that its number value has already been scored (Ex. a yahtzee of 2s can't be used as a joker if you haven't scored the 2s category yet). Additional Yahtzees will grant you 100 bonus points if you have scored 50 in the Yahtzee category.
